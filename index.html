About Hotel Njenka

Your Home Away From Home in Yaoundé

Located in the vibrant Esono district of Yaoundé, Hotel Njenka offers a perfect blend of modern comfort and traditional Cameroonian warmth. Since our establishment, we have been committed to providing exceptional hospitality to both business and leisure travelers.

Our prime location in Esono gives you easy access to Yaoundé's business districts, cultural attractions, and the city's best dining and entertainment options. Whether you're visiting for business or pleasure, Hotel Njenka provides the perfect base for your Yaoundé experience.
